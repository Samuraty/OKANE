Welcome to OKANE

OKANE (money in Japanese) is a website where you can exchange currency directly with other users. Create your account in Sign Up (or go to Log In if you already have one) and start exchanging your money today!

Create a new post or find what you need on Board. Specify the currency you have and the one you are looking for, and OKANE will show you all the users available for that deal, with exchange rates updated every hour.

Do you need to convert an amount into a different currency? Do you want to know the current exchange rates for different foreign currencies and their evolution in the last seven days? Take a look into the Currency Tools!

Manage your profile into My Profile section: modify your username, update your email, change your profile picture, edit or remove your posts... Keep your profile updated and get ready for the next currency exchange.
